Learn Erzya

Welcome!

The Erzya language is a Uralic language belonging to the Finno-Volgaic subgroup of the Finno-Ugric languages. It is closely related to the Moksha language, and in the past, these two were considered dialects of the so-called 'Mordvin language'. In the 2021 Russian census, around 40,000 people identified Erzya as their native language, with the majority living in the Republic of Mordovia. Erzya is one of the official languages of the Republic of Mordovia, alongside Russian and Moksha. While the official Erzya alphabet is identical to the Russian alphabet, scientific literature often uses various Latin-based systems to represent the language.

Erźań keleś te suomiń-ugrań semijanť suomiń-ravoń taradonteń sovića uraloń keľ. Sonenzë vesemede malaviks keleś – mokšonseś. Ikele sïnst kavonest loviź vejsëń isťa lemdeź „mordvań keleń“ kortavksoks. 2021 ijeń Rasejeń lovnomasonť malav 40 000 lomanť teškstïź erźań kelenť eś keleks, sïndest lamotne eriť Mordovija Respublikaso. Keleś ašti respublikanť služamoń keleks, ruzoń dï mokšoń keltneń jutkso. Pravań koŕas kemekstaź śormadoma kojeś koda ruzoń, jalateke naukań literaturaso sejedstë putoviť tevs lija, latinëń teškstnëń langs nežedeź sistemat.
