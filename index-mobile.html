Welcome!

The Erzya language (эрзянь кель, erzäń keľ), also Erzian or historically Arisa, is spoken by approximately 300,000 people in the northern, eastern and north-western parts of the Republic of Mordovia and adjacent regions of Chuvashia, Tatarstan, Bashkortostan, Penza, Samara, Nizhny Novgorod, Ulyanovsk etc.

The language belongs to the Mordvinic branch of the Uralic languages. Erzya is currently written using Cyrillic with no modifications to the variant used by the Russian language. In Mordovia, Erzya is co-official with Moksha and Russian.

Source: Wikipedia

Paročisë sovado!

Erzäń keľ te suomi-ugrań semijanť suomiń-ravoń taradonteń sovavtovicä uraloń keľ. Erzäń kelsë kortïť erzät. Rossijaso erzätnede 2010 ijeń lovnomasonť tekšstïź erzäń kelenť koda tirineks 36 726 lomať, sïnst ejstë lamotne eriť Mordovija Respublikaso. Erzäń kelenteń vesemede malaviks keleś — mokšoń keľ. Ikele keltneń loviź vejke mordvań keleń dialektëks.

Erzäń keleś ašti eslempeleń keleks Mordovijaso, ruzoń dï mokšoń keltneń marto. Eslempeleń śormadoma kojeś — ruzoń kirilicä, teke marto naukań literaturaso sejedstë noldtneviť tevs lamo lija, latinicä langs nežedeź sistemat.
