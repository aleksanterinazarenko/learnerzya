Conjugation

Infinitives

First infinitive

The dictionary form of a verb. Always ends with -мс: ловномс “to read”.


Second infinitive

Used with verbs like кармамс “to start”, молемс “to go”, тонавтнемс “to learn” etc.
It ends with -мо or -ме added to the verb base: ловномо “reading”, молеме “going”, тонавтнеме “learning”.
