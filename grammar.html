Conjugation

Infinitives

First infinitive

The dictionary form of a verb. Always ends with -мс (-ms): ловномс (lovnoms) “to read”.


Second infinitive

Used with verbs like кармамс (karmams) “to start”, молемс (moľems) “to go”, тонавтнемс (tonavtńems) “to learn” etc. Ends with -мо (-mo) (after back vowel) or -ме (-me) (after front vowel) added to the verb base: ловномо (lovnomo) “reading”, молеме (moľeme) “going”, тонавтнеме (tonavtńeme) “learning”.
