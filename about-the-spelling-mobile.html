Learn Erzya

The Romanization of the Erzya language used on this site is loosely based on the proposals found in the book by linguist Boris Serebrennikov, Историческая морфология мордовских языков and in Parlons mordve (erzya et mokša) by linguists Ksenija Djordjević and Jean-Léo Léonard.

The alphabet has 33 letters:

A a (Ä ä) B b C c Ć ć Č č D d Ď ď E e Ë ë F f G g H h I i Ï ï J j K k L l Ľ ľ M m N n Ń ń O o P p R r Ŕ ŕ S s Ś ś Š š T t Ť ť U u V v (W w) (X x) (Y y) Z z Ź ź Ž ž

All consonants after i, e, and the dialectal ä are always soft, while ë and ï make the preceding consonant hard, e.g., tejemstë /tʲejemstɛ/, lijalgadï /lʲijɑlɡɑdɨ/.

❗ Note that the transliteration tool supports only Sentence case and lower case. UPPER CASE will not work correctly!
