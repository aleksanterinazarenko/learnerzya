Conjugation

Infinitives

First infinitive

The dictionary form of a verb. Always ends with -ms: lovnoms “to read”.

Second infinitive

Used with verbs like karmams “to start”, molems “to go”, tonavtnems “to learn” etc.
It ends with -mo or -me added to the verb base: lovnomo “reading”, moleme “going”, tonavtneme “learning”.
